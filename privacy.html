Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of the Google Meet Link Generator. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information from our users. The extension operates locally within your browser and uses Google APIs solely to perform its intended functionality.

Use of Google APIs

The Google Meet Link Generator uses Google Calendar and Google Meet APIs to create meeting links and events. OAuth tokens are used for authentication and are handled securely within your browser. These tokens are never transmitted to third-party servers or stored permanently.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

Your Privacy Rights

As a user, you have the right to control your data. Since no data is collected or stored by this extension, there is no data to request, delete, or restrict.

Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
