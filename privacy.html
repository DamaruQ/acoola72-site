Privacy Policy

Welcome to Quick Meeting Links by acoola72 ("we", "our", or "the application"). This privacy policy explains how we access, use, and protect your data when using our service.

Google User Data We Access

Quick Meeting Links by acoola72 uses the following Google APIs to provide its functionality:

  • Google Calendar API: To view, create, and update calendar events on behalf of the user.
  • Google Calendar Events API: To access event details such as time, title, and participants for modifying or creating events.

We do not collect or store any personal information, calendar data, or other Google user data on external servers. All data is processed locally in your browser and remains under your control.

Data Protection Mechanisms

We ensure the security of your data through the following mechanisms:

  • OAuth 2.0 Authentication: Your data is accessed only with your explicit consent via Google's secure OAuth authentication.
  • Local Data Processing: All data is processed locally in your browser; no data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.
  • Minimal Permissions: The application only requests the minimum permissions necessary to perform its intended functionality (view and manage calendar events).

Data Retention

Quick Meeting Links does not retain any Google user data after the session ends. Your data is only accessed temporarily during active use of the application.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. All updates will be published on this page with a new effective date.
