Quick Meeting Links is a browser extension designed to simplify and speed up the creation of Google Meet links. It integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar to help users efficiently manage their meetings.

Click to read the complete guide

Key Features

- Automatic link generation: Google Meet links are created instantly for your events.
- Event customization: Adjust meeting details such as time, title, and participants.
- Calendar integration: Schedule meetings effortlessly with Google Calendar.

Data Security: All data is processed securely using Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication, ensuring your information remains private and protected.
